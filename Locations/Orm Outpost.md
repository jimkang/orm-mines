- 3 Sleeping Pods
- A bubblemaker (out of [[Bubbubbub]] but when it has it, can make [[Mottekian spossip]]s)

	- [[Rad]] suits, absorbs a total of 50 rad - 3 [[wire]]
	- A green [[cardkey]] - 7 wire

	- Water bottle - 1 wire
	- Scissors - 1 wire
